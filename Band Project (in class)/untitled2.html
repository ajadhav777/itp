SCREAM

About Us

Scream is a high-octane rock metal band that blends intense aggression with melodic complexity. Known for their blistering guitar riffs, thunderous drumming, and raw, guttural vocals, Scream brings a sound thats both heavy and captivating. Their music spans from dark, brooding anthems to intense, fast-paced breakdowns, creating a sonic experience thats as emotionally charged as it is powerful. Lyrically, Screams songs tackle themes of personal struggle, rebellion, and defiance against societal norms, reflecting their raw energy and rebellious spirit. With an unwavering commitment to pushing boundaries, the band has developed a loyal following, commanding every stage they play with a dynamic presence that resonates long after the last note fades. Whether its in the studio or on stage, Scream is a force of nature, an unrelenting storm of sound that leaves an indelible mark on anyone who experiences them.

Luca - Bass & Backing Vocals

The backbone of Scream's earth-shattering rhythm section, Luca brings thunder from below. His gritty basslines cut through the mix like a chainsaw, while his backing vocals add a sinister edge to the chaos. Stoic on stage but ferocious in tone, Luca anchors the storm with precision and power.

Ash - Drums & Lead Vocals

The heart of the beast, Ash commands both the kit and the mic. A rare force of nature, they slam out blast beats while tearing through lyrics with raw intensity. Ash's dual role turns every performance into a war cry-visceral, unrelenting, and unforgettable.

Riff - Guitar & Backing Vocals

Shredding with surgical precision, Riff lives up to the name. Their guitar work fuses brutality with melody, weaving intricate solos into the band's heavy assault. Offsetting the chaos with tight harmonies and backing screams, Riff lights the fuse and keeps the fire burning.
